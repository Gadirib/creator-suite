Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Creator Suite (“we”, “our”, “us”) handles information when you use the app.

1. Data We Process

  • TikTok account authorization data returned by TikTok OAuth (for example, access token and related identifiers).
  • Basic profile information provided by TikTok after consent (for example, display name and avatar).
  • Content metadata required for app features (for example, lists used in cleaner/content tools).

2. How We Use Data

  • To authenticate your TikTok connection.
  • To show connected-account information inside the app.
  • To enable features you explicitly trigger (for example, content/cleaner actions).

3. Storage and Security

  • We store only the minimum data needed for app functionality.
  • Sensitive credentials should be stored securely and never hardcoded in production apps.
  • We use reasonable technical measures to protect stored data.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with service providers needed to operate the app, or when required by law.

5. Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law. You can request account disconnection and deletion of app-linked data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. Contact us using the details below.

7. Children’s Privacy
